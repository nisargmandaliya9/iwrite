What do poems feel like?

Poem is the art that you write with your pencil. It is your thoughts that you make curves of. Your words are the colors that you fill in.

Poem is something that push your thoughts further. You watch something and if you find it mesmerizing, it gets stuck in your mind. If you put this feeling into words in a fency way, it becomes poem. And so, poems are the feelings written into words.

I agree with the quote by Carol Ann Duffy that says, "You can find poetry in your everyday life, your memory, in what people say on the bus, in the news, or just what's in your heart."
